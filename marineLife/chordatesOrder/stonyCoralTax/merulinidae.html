Taxonomy: Scleractinia: Merulinidae (Stony Corals)

Body Form: Merulinids have highly variable growth forms with sometimes striking colors.

Diet: Some have zooxanthellae and others are entirely heterotrophic. | Care Level: Difficult

Natural History: Most genus include species that are symbiotic and found on protected reef slopes and lagoons. They are unusually difficult to maintain in captivity, subject to bleaching and decline.
