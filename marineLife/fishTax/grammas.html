Taxonomy: Perciformes, Grammas (Grammatidae)

General Information: The grammas are a small group, encompassing only 11 species in 2 genera. They reach a maximum size of about 4 inches, have an incomplete or absent lateral line (organ of hearing), and are often found on deep slopes on rubble bottoms.

Diet: Most species are zooplankton feeders.

Final Note: The genus Lipogramma is generally a deepwater group that may require dim light. They are territorial and generally only one gramma per tank is recommended.

(Gramma melacara)

Japanese Basslet
(Liopropoma mitratum)

Royal Gramma
(Gramma loreto)

Swalesi Basslet
(Liopropoma swalesi)

Swissguard Basslet
(Liopropoma rubre)
